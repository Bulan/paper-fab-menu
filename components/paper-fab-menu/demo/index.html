An example of <paper-fab-menu>:

Just wrap a couple of paper-fab with a paper-fab-menu and they will all be put in a "Speed Dial" menu.
The first paper-fab will be the main FAB. 

    <paper-fab-menu>
      <paper-fab icon="save" title="Save"></paper-fab>
      <paper-fab icon="favorite" title="A tooltip"></paper-fab>
      <paper-fab icon="rowing" title="Don't swim, Row!"></paper-fab>
    </paper-fab-menu>
